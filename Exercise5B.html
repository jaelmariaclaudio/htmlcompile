MY FAVORITE DISH

Bicol Express

Bicol Express is my pure comfort food. Its taste is from the authenticity of Bicol. It definitely deserves to carry the name of the province of Bicol, known in the Philippines for a lot of things such as the perfect coned, Mayon volcano, Pili nuts and, of course, for their love of spicy food.The taste of this dish will surely get your tongue up on fire. Its rich, creamy, and spicy taste is really good and satisfying. This dish is served with steamed rice for a hearty and big flavored meal.

INGREDIENTS OF BICOL EXPRESS

  • Pork
  • 3 pieces long green pepper (siling pansigang) cut into thin pieces
  • pieces bird’s eye chili (siling labuyo) or Thai chili cut into small pieces
  • Shrimp Paste (uncooked)
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 ¼ cups water
  • Salt

HOW TO COOK BICOL EXPRESS

  • Heat oil in a pot and then add the onion and garlic. Saute until the onion is soften.
  • Add the pork and the ground black pepper. Saute until the color of the dish turns light brown.
  • Add 1 1/2 cup of water and then let it boil. Continue cooking it in medium heat with cover until the pork is tender and the water has evaporated.
  • Prepare the gata. Combine it with lukewarm water. Stir until it has completely dissolved.
  • Add the gata mixture to the pot and continue cooking it until it will boil. Cover the pot and continue cooking it in medium heat until the liquid has reduced leaving only half of the mixture. Stir it occasionally.
  • Add salt depending on your taste according to the recipe and transfer it to a bowl once you’re done cooking.
  • Serve it with rice and enjoy your meal.
